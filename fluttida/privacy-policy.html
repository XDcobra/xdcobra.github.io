2. Overview

Fluttida is a developer tool app for testing different network stacks and SSL pinning configurations. You can use Fluttida without registration and without creating a user account. This privacy policy explains which data is processed when you use the app and for which purposes.

3. Data collection and processing

3.1 Data processed locally by the app

The app stores the following information exclusively on your device:

  • SSL pinning configurations: Your saved pinning settings are stored only locally on your device.
  • App settings: Preferences and configurations for network tests (e.g. selected stacks, target URLs, header configurations).

These locally stored data are not accessed by us as the provider and are not transmitted to our own servers. We only process personal data if you contact us directly, for example via email.

3.3 Network requests and target servers

The app sends HTTP(S) requests to URLs that you enter or select in order to test different network stacks and compare their behaviour. The content, destination and frequency of these requests are determined solely by you as the user.

The resulting request and response data (including headers and response bodies) are processed only on your device, for example to display results and logs within the app. These data are not stored or logged on our own servers.

Please note that the privacy policies of the respective providers apply to the target servers you choose (e.g. APIs, websites, your own test systems). You are responsible for ensuring that you do not send unlawful or sensitive personal data to third-party servers.

No additional “dangerous” Android permissions (such as access to contacts, camera, microphone or device storage) are used, unless explicitly stated in a future version of the app.
