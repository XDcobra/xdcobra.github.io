2. Description of the App

Offline AI Voice Toolkit is an offline speech and audio processing toolkit that allows users to experiment with technologies such as speech-to-text, text-to-speech, voice activity detection, diarization, audio enhancement, and source separation.

All audio processing is performed locally on your device using downloaded speech models. The application does not upload audio data to external servers. No user account is required to use the app. Some optional services such as advertising or crash reporting may require an internet connection, but they do not process or upload your audio data.
