2. Description of the App

Voice Lab: STT, TTS & More is an offline speech and audio processing toolkit that allows users to experiment with technologies such as speech-to-text, text-to-speech, translation, voice cloning, voice activity detection, diarization, audio enhancement, and source separation.

Core speech processing is performed locally on your device using downloaded speech models. The application does not upload your audio, transcripts, or synthesized output to our servers for processing. No login account is required to use the app. Some optional services such as advertising, subscription validation, anonymous usage metering, crash reporting, or model downloads may require an internet connection, but they do not process or upload your session audio content as described in our Privacy Policy.

Voice cloning is intended only for voices you own or have explicit permission to use. You must not use voice cloning to impersonate others, create deceptive deepfakes, or mislead third parties.
